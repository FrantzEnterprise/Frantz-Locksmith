Why I Do What I Do

I've been working with safes since 1985 — over 40 years in the industry. I opened Frantz Locksmith Service in 2005, and safes quickly became my passion and specialty. I treat every safe — whether it's an antique heirloom or a modern gun safe — with the same care and respect.

As I've narrowed my focus over the years, I do what I do best: safe services. I'm a preferred technician for manufacturers like Liberty Safe, Fort Knox, Stealth Safe, Superior Safe, and National Safe — handling warranty work, fire-damaged safe openings, and probate situations. I've also opened GSA government containers and worked with supermarket chains across Northern California. I still do light locksmith work for my clients, especially if they need lock service as part of a safe installation or rekey after a move-in.

What you get with me is experience. I've seen every kind of safe problem, and I know how to fix it without causing damage. I'm mobile — I bring my tools and knowledge to your home or business.
