⚠️ Safe Opening Disclaimer

Safe opening is a specialized skill that involves risks. By engaging our services, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

  • Manipulation (non-destructive opening) is attempted on every safe where possible. However, not all locks can be manipulated.
  • Precision drilling may be required. All drilling is performed at the manufacturer's recommended entry points when known, and repair is included in the service.
  • In rare cases, a safe may have internal conditions (relock triggers, hardened plates, additional barriers) that were not disclosed by the manufacturer or owner. These may require additional time or techniques.
  • We are not responsible for the contents of the safe. Valuable items should be inventoried by the owner prior to service.
  • For fire safes that have been through a fire, the structure may be compromised. Opening may cause additional damage. This will be discussed before work begins.
