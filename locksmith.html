Locksmith Services for My Safe Clients

If you've called me for a safe opening or repair and need lock work done too — I'm happy to help. I offer light locksmith services for residential, commercial, and automotive needs. It's a straightforward part of the business I've been doing since 1985.

Residential Locksmith

  • Lock Rekey — Move into a new home? I'll rekey all your locks so one key works them all.
  • Deadbolt Installation — Schlage, Kwikset, Baldwin, Medeco high security — I carry quality brands.
  • Digital Locks — Touch screen and push-button keypad locks. No more fumbling for keys.
  • Lockout Service — Locked out? I'll get you back in, non-destructively, usually in under 15 minutes.
  • Key Duplication — Need a spare? I can cut standard keys and high-security restricted keys.

Commercial Locksmith

  • Master Key Systems — One key opens what it should, nothing more. Simple to complex hierarchies.
  • Exit Hardware — Panic bars, alarm bars, push paddles, and door closers.
  • Access Control — Electric strikes, magnetic locks, card readers, and fob systems.
  • High-Security — Medeco cylinders, restricted key control, interchangeable cores.

Automotive Locksmith

  • Car Key Duplication — Standard keys and laser-cut (sidewinder) keys.
  • Transponder Programming — Chip keys for modern vehicles — programmed on-site.
  • Lockout Service — Keys locked in your car? Mobile service to your location.
  • Broken Key Extraction — Key broke off in ignition or door? I'll get it out.

💡 Straight Talk

Safes are my main focus, and that's what I do best. But if you need lock work as part of a safe job — rekeying after moving into a home with a safe, installing a deadbolt near your safe, or just needing a hand with keys — I'm glad to help. Give me a call and I'll be upfront about what I can do.
