Safe Opening

Forgot the combination? Electronic keypad stopped working? Lost the key? Don't force it — call me. Forcing a safe can cause thousands in damage. I use precision techniques to open safes non-destructively.

  • Combination lock manipulation — I work the dial to find the numbers
  • Electronic keypad failures — bypass and replacement
  • Drill opening — only when necessary, and properly repaired afterward
  • Lost key override — key-operated safe lock service
  • GSA container service — certified on X-07, X-08, X-09, X-10

Safe Repair & Maintenance

High-end safes can be repaired after opening — saving you the cost, time, and hassle of replacing it. I service all standard makes and models.

  • Combination lock service and replacement
  • Digital lock upgrade — replace old dials with modern keypad locks
  • Handle, hinge, and bolt work repair
  • Relocker service (the secondary locking mechanism that triggers on attack)
  • Annual safe maintenance and inspection

Digital Lock Upgrades

Old dial combination taking too long? Upgrade to a digital keypad. Quick entry, easy combination changes, and no more fumbling with a dial. I install and program all standard digital safe locks.

💡 A Tip On Safe Combinations

Some safes unlock turning left, others unlock turning right. If your first attempt fails, try the opposite direction. Also — some safes have a small key that locks the dial. Make sure you're not missing that key before calling. But if you're stuck, I'm just a phone call away.
